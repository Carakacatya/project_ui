SOY BRIGHT! Gel Moisturizer is a revolutionary skincare product that combines the benefits of a hydrating moisturizer and a makeup primer in one elegant formula. This lightweight gel delivers intense hydration while creating the perfect canvas for makeup application.

The unique formula features our proprietary Soy Bright Complexâ„¢, which combines soy extract, niacinamide, and triple-weight hyaluronic acid to deliver multiple benefits:

  • Brightening: Soy extract and niacinamide work together to even skin tone and reduce the appearance of dark spots and hyperpigmentation.
  • Hydrating: Triple-weight hyaluronic acid provides multi-dimensional hydration by targeting different layers of the skin.
  • Smoothing: The gel texture instantly smooths skin texture, minimizing the appearance of pores and fine lines.
  • Priming: Creates the perfect base for makeup, helping foundation and other products apply more smoothly and last longer.

The oil-free, non-comedogenic formula is suitable for all skin types, including sensitive and acne-prone skin. It absorbs quickly without leaving a greasy residue, making it perfect for use both morning and night.

Our products are formulated without parabens, sulfates, phthalates, mineral oil, and artificial fragrances. We prioritize clean, effective ingredients that deliver real results.

Directions:

  1. Cleanse: Start with clean, dry skin. Apply your toner and any treatment serums.
  2. Apply: Take a pea-sized amount of SOY BRIGHT! Gel Moisturizer and warm it between your fingertips.
  3. Massage: Gently massage into your face and neck using upward, circular motions until fully absorbed.
  4. Follow up: In the morning, follow with sunscreen. In the evening, this can be your final step.

Pro Tips:

  • For an extra boost of hydration, apply to slightly damp skin.
  • Can be used as a 5-minute hydrating mask by applying a thicker layer and tissue off excess after 5 minutes.
  • Store in a cool place for an extra refreshing application.
  • Perfect for use under makeup as a primer - wait 60 seconds after application before applying foundation.
