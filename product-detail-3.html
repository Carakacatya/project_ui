Directions:

  1. Cleanse: Start with clean, dry skin.
  2. Apply: Pour a small amount onto a cotton pad or directly into palms.
  3. Pat: Gently pat the essence onto your face and neck until fully absorbed.
  4. Follow up: Continue with your serums, moisturizer, and sunscreen (during the day).

Pro Tips:

  • For an extra boost of hydration, apply multiple thin layers (the "7-skin method").
  • Can be used as a quick hydrating mask by soaking cotton pads and placing on dry areas for 5 minutes.
  • Store in the refrigerator for an extra refreshing application.
  • Mix with your favorite serum for enhanced absorption.
